- a medical app

- a diagnostic service

The app content, plans, reminders, trackers, and product guidance are provided as general wellness support only.

## 3. Data We Collect

Depending on how the app is used, we may collect:
- Account data: email address, auth identifier, sign-in method
- Profile data: selected goals, onboarding answers, hydration and sleep baselines, optional starting weight, personal notes
- Wellness tracking data: water intake, sleep entries, weight entries, mood entries, daily notes, streaks, adherence/checklist status
- Reminder data: notification preferences, reminder schedules, push token when notifications are enabled
- Consent records: disclaimer, privacy, and terms acceptance timestamps and versions
- Product interaction data: outbound click events, product page opens, source screen
- Technical data: crash information, diagnostics, app version, device/OS information, and service logs reasonably required for security, support, and analytics

## 4. How We Use Data

We use data to:
- create and maintain the user’s wellness routine
- personalize plan length, content, and product categories
- sync user data across devices for signed-in accounts
- provide reminders and notification functionality
- understand core product usage and improve app quality
- maintain security, investigate bugs, and respond to support requests
- document user consent and legal acknowledgements

## 5. Guest Mode

Some app functionality may be available without account creation.

When guest mode is used:
- some data may remain only on the device
- cloud sync may be unavailable
- some features may be limited or behave differently from signed-in mode

## 6. External Product Links

The app may open external product pages, shop pages, or cart flows on a third-party website.

When a user leaves the app:
- the third-party site’s privacy policy and terms apply
- purchases, payments, shipping, and merchant operations are handled outside the app
- we may log an outbound click event for analytics and support purposes
