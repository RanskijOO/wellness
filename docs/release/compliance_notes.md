# Wellness Compliance Notes

This document is intended to keep product, design, marketing, and release work aligned with store-safe wellness positioning.

## Approved Positioning

Use language such as:
- wellness support
- lifestyle support
- habit support
- hydration routine
- sleep routine support
- self-care consistency
- general product guidance
- optional routine support

## Avoid

Do not use language such as:
- diagnose
- treat
- cure
- prevent disease
- therapy
- prescription alternative
- medical-grade
- clinically proven for a disease outcome
- guaranteed result

## Product Guidance Rules

Product guidance in this repository should be framed as:
- general wellness support
- optional routine support
- general lifestyle context

Do not imply:
- a product will resolve symptoms
- a product is appropriate for a disease or condition
- a product replaces medication, physician guidance, or clinical care

## Metadata Rules

Store metadata, screenshots, and reviewer notes should:
- match real app functionality
- keep non-medical positioning consistent
- show disclaimer/legal availability
- avoid false or misleading supplement claims
- avoid regulated/clinical wording unless you truly hold the required approvals

## Data and Consent Rules

The app should clearly disclose:
- what user-entered wellness data is stored
- whether guest mode is available
- whether reminders are optional
- that product links open external sites
- that privacy policy, terms, and disclaimer are available in app

## Platform-Specific Submission Notes

### Apple

Key review themes relevant to this project:
- accurate metadata
- full reviewer access
- data minimization
- respect for user consent
- if account creation is offered, in-app account deletion is expected in the shipping app

### Google Play

Key review themes relevant to this project:
- Health apps declaration
- public privacy policy URL
- consistent Data safety disclosures
- clear non-medical disclaimer when the app is not a medical device
- no misleading or harmful health functionality
- no false or misleading supplement claims

## Future Feature Red Flags

Re-review compliance before shipping any of the following:
- symptom checker flows
- medication or dosage suggestions
- medical interpretation of metrics
- claims tied to specific conditions or diseases
- Health Connect / HealthKit features that expand sensitive data use
- AI chat outputs that could be interpreted as medical advice
