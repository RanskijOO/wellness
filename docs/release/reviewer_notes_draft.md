## Shared Reviewer Summary

Aloe Wellness Coach is a wellness and lifestyle support app.

It is not:
- a medical device
- a diagnostic tool
- a treatment service
- a disease prevention app

The app helps users:
- choose wellness goals
- follow 7 / 14 / 21-day habit-oriented plans
- track water, sleep, weight, mood, notes, and adherence
- view general product category guidance
- open external product pages in a webview or external browser
