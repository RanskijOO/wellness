# PRD — Aloe Wellness Coach

## 1. Product vision

Aloe Wellness Coach is a cross-platform mobile app for iOS and Android that
helps users build healthy daily wellness routines with hydration tracking, habit
support, progress monitoring, and safe product guidance using a catalog of
wellness products.

## 2. Problem

Users often buy wellness products without consistency, routine structure,
reminders, or progress tracking. They need a simple app that helps them:

- choose a goal
- follow a structured plan
- track daily actions
- see progress
- access suggested products safely

## 3. Product positioning

This is a wellness and lifestyle support app. It is not a medical app. It does
not diagnose, treat, cure, or replace professional care.

## 4. Primary user groups

- people who want a daily wellness routine
- users who want hydration reminders
- users who want 7 / 14 / 21-day guidance
- users who want simple progress tracking
- users interested in product guidance within a wellness context

## 5. MVP feature scope

- onboarding
- email auth + guest mode
- dashboard
- goals selection
- rule-based recommendations
- product cards
- 7 / 14 / 21-day plans
- trackers: water, sleep, weight, mood, notes
- reminders
- profile
- legal screens
- outbound product links

## 6. Out of scope for MVP

- medical recommendations
- complex supplement dosage logic
- in-app purchases for product catalog
- doctor chat
- disease-specific flows
- community feed

## 7. Success metrics

- onboarding completion rate
- day-1 activation
- 7-day retention
- percentage of users completing first plan day
- reminder opt-in rate
- outbound product click-through rate
- streak length
- daily log completion rate

## 8. Key screens

- splash
- onboarding welcome
- goals selection
- questionnaire
- consent
- auth
- dashboard
- product list
- product detail
- plan overview
- daily plan detail
- trackers
- analytics
- profile
- settings
- privacy policy
- terms
- disclaimer
- support

## 9. Recommendation logic

Inputs:

- selected goal
- water baseline
- sleep baseline
- mood
- adherence level
- optional preferences

Outputs:

- plan assignment
- recommended product categories
- suggested daily actions
- reminders frequency

## 10. Compliance

Use wellness wording only. Provide legal screens and consent flow. Collect
minimal user data. Keep architecture ready for future policy requirements.
